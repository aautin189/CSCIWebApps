Is Computer Science for Me?

What am I motivated to learn in this program?



1. Tradeoffs


What are you willing to sacrifice in order to get the results you want?

With any business decision, there are tradeoffs. Rarely do we find ourselves with the ideal combination of ingredients. Most the time, we have to sacrifice one thing to get another. That's that I mean by tradeoffs.

I want to know what these ingredients are. I want to know which things I can combine and which I cannot. I want to know the relative costs/benefits to each of those ingredients. I want to know how to make smart tradeoff decisions with technology.
